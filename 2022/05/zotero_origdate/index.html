Original publication date in Zotero

2 May 2022

Fill ‘Extra’ field in Zotero with original-date: (year) to include original publication date in a reference.

For example: English edition of Sport und Arbeit by Bero Rigauer.

Zotero creates a correct reference depending on style:

This setting also works for exporting citations to BibLaTeX using Better BibTeX extension:
