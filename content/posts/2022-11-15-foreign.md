---

APA style requires providing a translated title for a foreign-language
sources. To produce a correct citation with Zotero, type a translated
title into the "Extra" field with the `medium:` prefix.

**Examples:**

Brümmer, K. (2019). Spielsysteme, Matchpläne, Spielanalysen: Über
Praktiken und Medien des Kontingenzmanagements im gegenwärtigen
Fußball [Systems of play, match plans, game analyses: On practices and
media of contingency management in contemporary football]. _Sport und
Gesellschaft, 16_(3), 266–300. https://doi.org/10.1515/sug-2019-0017

Coulon, A. (1998). Le football comme spectacle: Le plaisir de
l’arbitraire [Football as a spectacle: the pleasure of arbitrariness].
_Communications, 67_(1), 25–32. https://doi.org/10.3406/comm.1998.2013
