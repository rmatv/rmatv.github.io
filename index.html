Roman Matvienko

I am a PhD student in Sociology at the Chinese University of Hong Kong.

I do a video-based ethnomethodological study of embodied interaction in team sports. My current work focusses on the projectability of action in playing association football and the interactional difficulties that arise from the fact that every action by a player is, in principle, visible not only to their teammates, but also to their opponents.
